Just like telephone scams and tech support scams / virus scams, email scams prey on the elderly and the less technologically savvy.
-----------------------------------------------------

What is phishing?

Phishing is the fraudulent practice of sending emails purporting to be from reputable companies in order to trick victims into revealing personal information, such as passwords and credit card information.


How to protect yourself?

- Do NOT open any emails that you don't recognize the sender.

- Do NOT download any file or click any link from an email in which you do not recognize the sender.
